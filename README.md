# Deepfield Transmissions - Wallpaper Pack v1.0 💠

Official high-resolution background collection from the Deepfield Transmissions project.

## About
Deepfield Transmissions is a research initiative documenting the evolution of global infrastructure, AI, and culture. These images were captured during dispatches to Tokyo, Chennai, Amsterdam, Seoul, London, and other strategic nodes.

## Content
This branch contains 22 high-resolution backgrounds (PNG format).

### Locations & Themes
- **Tokyo:** Robotics, urban density, and night-markets.
- **Chennai:** Software hubs and traditional coffee culture.
- **Amsterdam:** Open-source AI and canal-side data centers.
- **Seoul:** High-speed networks and tech-noir aesthetics.
- **Bangkok:** AI-augmented street food and commerce.
- **London:** Tech City infrastructure.
- **Frankfurt:** Finance and data interchange.
- **And more...**
